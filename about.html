About This Website

The Emotion Analyzer is a simple web-based tool designed to help you reflect on your emotions. Based on short psychological indicators, it helps you identify whether you’re feeling happy, sad, anxious, or angry.

⚠️ This tool is not a substitute for professional mental health advice. If you are struggling or feeling distressed, please consult a qualified therapist or counselor.
